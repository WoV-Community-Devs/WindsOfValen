Overview

This calculator implements the full combat formulas from Winds of Valen. It computes hit chance, damage, DPS, time to kill, and kills per hour for any player loadout against any monster.

Enemy defence affects accuracy only (whether you hit). There is no damage reduction on monsters — once you hit, your full damage applies.

Player Inputs

These are the values the calculator uses from your loadout:

  • Attack Level (1–99) — Your attack skill level. Used for both accuracy and damage rolls.
  • Archery Level (1–99) — Used instead of Attack Level when a bow is equipped.
  • Equipped Accuracy — Total Slash or Pierce accuracy from all gear. Affects accuracy only.
  • Weapon Damage — Sum of Quick Damage + Heavy Damage from the weapon tooltip. Affects damage only.
  • Attack Speed — Speed value on the weapon tooltip (1.0–5.0). Affects both damage and time per attack.
  • Melee / Range Power — Total power from all equipped gear. Boosted by gems. Affects damage only.
Enemy Inputs

These values are loaded automatically when a monster is selected:

  • Defence Level — The monster's defence skill level.
  • Pierce Defence — Defence stat used against pierce weapons.
  • Slash Defence — Defence stat used against slash weapons.
  • Health — Monster's total HP, used for time-to-kill calculations.
  • Type — Boss or Mob. Bosses have a 15s respawn timer.
Accuracy Calculation

Roll Values

MaxAccuracyRoll = (attackLevel + 8) × (equippedAccuracy + 32)

MaxDefenceRoll = (defenceLevel + 8) × (equippedDefence + 16)

Constants: 8 = base level offset, 32 = base accuracy bonus, 16 = base defence bonus.

How Combat Rolls Work

Each attack, the game rolls a random number from 0 to MaxAccuracyRoll and another from 0 to MaxDefenceRoll. If the accuracy roll is strictly greater than the defence roll, the attack hits.

Hit Chance Formula

If MaxAccuracyRoll > MaxDefenceRoll:

hitChance = 1 − (MaxDefenceRoll + 2) / (2 × (MaxAccuracyRoll + 1))

Otherwise:

hitChance = MaxAccuracyRoll / (2 × MaxDefenceRoll + 1)

Hit chance is always between 0 and 1 (exclusive) — never exactly 0% or 100%.

Weapon Type Matters

Slash weapons use Slash Accuracy vs enemy Slash Defence. Pierce weapons (including bows) use Pierce Accuracy vs enemy Pierce Defence. Archery weapons also substitute Archery Level for Attack Level.

Damage Calculation

Constants

  • BASE_DAMAGE = 5
  • BASE_EFFECTIVE_LEVEL = 8
  • EFFECTIVE_LEVEL_DAMAGE_MULTIPLIER = 1.1
  • BASE_EQUIPMENT_DAMAGE = 30
  • EQUIPMENT_POWER_STRENGTH = 0.333333
  • DAMAGE_DIVIDER = 50

Max Hit Formula

effectiveAttackLevel = attackLevel + 8

weaponDamageBonus = weaponDamage + 30

flatDamageBonus = meleePower × 0.333333 × attackSpeed

maxHit = floor(5 + (effectiveAttackLevel × 1.1 × (weaponDamageBonus + flatDamageBonus)) / 50)

Weapon Damage is the sum of Quick Damage and Heavy Damage stats. Melee/Range Power is boosted by gems before this calculation.

Damage Range

  • Min Hit = floor(maxHit × 0.5)
  • Avg Hit = floor(maxHit × 0.75)
  • Max Hit = maxHit

Every hit rolls uniformly between Min Hit and Max Hit.

Kills Per Hour

KPH = 3600 / (secondsToKill + 15)

All monsters (bosses and regular mobs) use a 15s respawn timer.

XP Per Hour

Combat XP Multiplier

CombatMult = 1 + (monsterLevel × 0.01)

Base XP Per Kill

BaseXP = monsterHP × CombatMult

XP Distribution

  • Combat XP per kill = BaseXP × 3/4
  • Hitpoints XP per kill = BaseXP / 4

XP Per Hour

XP/Hr = XP per kill × Kills Per Hour

Kills per hour accounts for the 15s respawn timer.

Two-Handed Weapons & Bows

Two-handed weapons (greatswords, axes, bows) disable the shield slot.

Bows additionally require arrows to be equipped and use Archery Level instead of Attack Level for both accuracy and damage calculations. Bows use Pierce Accuracy (from arrows and other gear).

What Affects What
  • Attack / Archery Level → Accuracy (hit chance) + Damage (max hit)
  • Equipped Accuracy → Accuracy only
  • Weapon Damage → Damage only
  • Attack Speed → Damage (flat bonus) + Time per attack
  • Melee / Range Power → Damage (flat bonus)
  • Enemy Defence → Accuracy only (no damage reduction on monsters)
